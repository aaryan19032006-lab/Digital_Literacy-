## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.
- Do not approve unknown “Collect” or payment requests on apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm.  
- Always verify the identity of callers or messages asking for money or personal information.
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links received via SMS, WhatsApp, or email.
- Do not install screen-sharing or unknown apps suggested by strangers.  
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts.
- Regularly check your bank account and UPI transaction history for unauthorized activity.  
- Use only official apps downloaded from trusted sources like Google Play Store or App Store.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or financial transactions.
- Immediately report any fraud to the National Cyber Crime Portal (https://cybercrime.gov.in) or call the helpline number 1930.
